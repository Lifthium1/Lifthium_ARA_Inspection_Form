通知Notice
1
本報告根據《曳引式升降機風險評估指引》制定。This report is prepared in accordance with the Guideline on Risk Assessment for Traction Lifts.
2
本報告須以電腦印刷或原子筆填寫。字跡需清晰、整潔。This report must be completed by computer printout or in ballpoint pen, with clear and legible handwriting.
3
如沒有風險評估員簽署，此報告為無效。This report is invalid without the signature of the risk assessor.
4
升降機負責人如對評估報告及建議有異議或其他意見的，應於收到本報告之日起 15 個曆日內向評估機構提出書面意見。If the responsible person disagrees with or has other comments on the report and recommendations, they should submit written comments to the assessment organisation within 15 calendar days from the date of receipt.
5
本報告及其建議僅適用於被評估升降機的現狀；如評估後升降機及其使用環境有任何變化，本評估報告中的相關條款及結論可能不再適用。This report and its recommendations apply only to the current condition of the assessed lift. If the lift or its operating environment changes after the assessment, the relevant terms and conclusions may no longer apply.
6
在任何情況下，若需引用本報告中的結果或數據，均應保持原意，不得添加、修改、偽造或否認事實。Under any circumstances, when citing the results or data in this report, the original meaning must be preserved; no addition, modification, falsification or denial of facts is permitted.
7
本報告需一式三份，評估機構留存一份，升降機負責人留存兩份。This report shall be made in triplicate: one copy retained by the assessment organisation and two copies by the responsible person.
